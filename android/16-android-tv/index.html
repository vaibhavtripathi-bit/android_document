Android TV

Section 16 — Leanback UI, D-pad navigation, TV app architecture, content discovery, streaming integrations — for Hotstar, Sony LIV, Amazon Prime TV, YouTube TV interviews

Why This Section Matters

Android TV runs on Smart TVs, streaming sticks (Chromecast with Google TV), and set-top boxes. Hotstar, Amazon Prime, Sony LIV, and YouTube have dedicated TV apps. At L6, you need to understand the TV platform's unique constraints: no touch input, 10-foot UI design, limited RAM, D-pad focus navigation, and Leanback library architecture.
