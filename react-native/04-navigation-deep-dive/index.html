Series Focus

Navigation is one of the most complex aspects of React Native development. Unlike the web where the browser manages history and URL routing, mobile apps must manage their own navigation stacks, handle platform-specific transitions, and integrate with OS-level deep linking. This series covers the full depth needed for Staff-level interviews.
